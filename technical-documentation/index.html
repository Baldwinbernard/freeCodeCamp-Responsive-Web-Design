Introduction

CashToken Rewards Africa is a Nigerian RewardTech company founded in April 2018, pioneering Africa's first Cash Reward as-a-Service (CRaaS) platform. Its mission is to transform customer loyalty by offering rewards and an automatic ticket to win up to millions in the weekly national draw.

The company aims to enhance consumer financial empowerment and business profitability through its innovative reward system.

# Example: Customer receives CashToken via SMS SMS: "MTN has gifted you 1 CashToken! You have N35 in your wallet and a ticket to the weekly draw."
CashToken System

The CashToken is an electronic reward commodity that provides guaranteed instant cash (N35 per token) and entry into weekly National Consumer Draws with prizes from N5,000 to N100 million.

Customers earn CashTokens by purchasing goods or services from partnered businesses, accessible via *6700# or the CashToken platform.

# USSD Code to check CashToken balance Dial *6700# -> Select "Reward Wallet" -> Check Balance
  • Guaranteed cash-back of N35 per CashToken
  • Weekly draw entry for life-changing rewards
Partnerships

CashToken has partnered with over 2,000 businesses, including MTN Nigeria, Airtel, 9mobile, and Mastercard, to deliver rewards across various sectors.

A notable collaboration with the Nigerian government supports a national reward program, incentivizing purchases nationwide.

# Example: Partnership integration Merchant sets purchase threshold on CashToken Website. Customer earns CashToken for meeting threshold.
Technology and Model

The Universal Gamification of Sales and Consumer Rewards Program (UGSCRP) drives CashToken's model, optimizing loyalty through a Universal Cash Reward as-a-Service (UCRaaS) system.

Businesses can integrate via USSD (*6700#) or the CashToken platform, setting reward thresholds to automate customer rewards.

# Merchant setup example Login to cashtokenrewards.com Set Purchase Threshold: N35 = 1 CashToken
  • USSD integration for accessibility
  • Web platform for merchant management
Impact and Expansion

In five years, CashToken rewarded nearly one million Nigerians with $10 million in CashTokens, boosting consumer economies and business revenues.

With a $100 million commitment from Global Emerging Markets, CashToken is expanding to Ghana, Kenya, and other African countries.
